About Sheek Solutions

Full Company Bio

Sheek Solutions is a premium corporate audio-visual production and labor firm trusted by executives, associations, and venues to deliver shows that simply don’t miss. We design, staff, and operate end-to-end live events—from ballrooms and boardrooms to large-scale broadcasts—bringing calm control and exacting detail to every cue.

Led by founder Anthony Walton, a Barco Event Master–certified technical director, our teams include show callers, TDs, video engineers, graphics/playback ops, streaming specialists, and stage managers who speak the language of live. We handle preproduction, run-of-show, and on-site operations: switching, LED/projection, graphics, cameras, streaming/recording, comms, and stage management—plus labor brokering and crew leadership for seamless execution.

Based in Arizona and operating nationwide, Sheek Solutions is known for white-glove service, clear communication, and on-time strikes. When the stakes are high and the timeline is tight, we’re the team that makes it look effortless.
